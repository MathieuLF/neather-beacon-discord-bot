## PokéAPI and Pokémon assets

NeatherBeacon can request public Pokémon data and artwork at runtime through PokéAPI.

- Pokémon data and artwork are not authored by this project.
- Pokémon artwork is cached locally at runtime only.
- Cached PokéAPI files under `runtime/pokedex-cache` are generated runtime data and must not be committed.
- Public repository content should not include downloaded Pokémon artwork unless the publisher has separately confirmed the right to distribute it.
